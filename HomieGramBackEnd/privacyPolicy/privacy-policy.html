Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 4, 2025

At HomiGram, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use HomiGram:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, and phone number
  • User profile data, including location, bio, and room preferences
  • Device data such as device type and OS version
  • Chat messages and comments within the app
  • Bookmarks and interactions with rooms or properties

2. How We Collect Information

Information is collected when you:

  • Register or update your user account
  • Browse rooms or interact with content
  • Send messages or comments
  • Bookmark or rent properties
  • Use app features that request access to location or media (with permission)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize your app experience
  • Match students with rental opportunities
  • Enable communication between users, landlords, and administrators
  • Maintain and improve the app’s functionality and security
  • Send important updates and notifications

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Other users (e.g., landlords seeing your name/contact when booking)
  • Third-party service providers for hosting, analytics, or messaging (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • Law enforcement or authorities when legally required

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time via in-app settings or by contacting us. We will delete your information unless retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We use secure servers, encryption, and access control to protect your data. While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

HomiGram does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app implies acceptance of the changes.
